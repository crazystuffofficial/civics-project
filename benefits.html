Benefits of Civics Education

Importants of civics, and how it can benefit you

Civics education can help many students learn about how the government works, and knowing about the government comes with many benefits. Here is what we need to know in order to grasp these benefits:

Rights: We need to know our rights as citizens.
Elections: We need to increase voter participation.
Laws: We need to know how laws are made and how to add new ones and delete bad ones.
